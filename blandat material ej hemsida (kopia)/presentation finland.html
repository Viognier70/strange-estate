Who We Are

OUR
MISSION

Strange Estate rescues high-quality wines that would otherwise go to waste — and transforms them into entirely new products through careful blending, craft, and creative precision.

We are not a vineyard. We are a creative upcycling winery.

LESS WASTE· BETTER WINE· UPCYCLED· FANTASTIC· THE PLANET IS OUR DOMAIN· SMAK INTE URSPRUNG· LESS WASTE· BETTER WINE· UPCYCLED· FANTASTIC·
The Problem
500 million wine bottles equivalent discarded globally every year. High-quality wine goes to waste due to surplus and distribution inefficiencies.
Our Answer
We identify surplus wines in the supply chain, blend them into new products, and release them under the Strange Estate portfolio.
The Result
Premium, sustainable beverages with measurable CO₂ savings — and a story consumers genuinely want to tell.
04 / 15
Process

THE METHOD

Strange Estate works with wine that already exists. Rather than producing new wine from vineyards, the project focuses on wines already present in the distribution system that risk remaining unused.
RESCUE
Identify surplus wines that remain unused in the supply chain. Wines are sourced from distributors and importers across Europe.
BLEND
Through careful sensory analysis and blending we create balanced wines that highlight fruit, acidity, and structure.
RELEASE
The resulting blends are released as new wines within the Strange Estate series — certified and quality controlled.

Philosophy

ART &
SCIENCE

Inspired by Denis Diderot's philosophy — integrating scientific sensory analysis with artistic blending craft.

Developed in collaboration with Campus Grythyttan, Örebro University. Our Double Grip Analysis model enables precise, measurable quality decisions alongside creative expression.

Research Partner
Campus Grythyttan · Örebro University
Sommelerie & Framtidens Måltider
SCIENTIFIC APPROACH
Sensory analysis, Double Grip methodology, 4-tier sorting model. Precise, measurable insights that guide every blend decision.
ARTISTIC APPROACH
Creative blending, editorial identity, music pairing. Capturing the intangible and emotional dimensions of wine experience.
"The interaction between science and art mirrors Diderot's vision — where both structured analysis and creative insight are essential to capturing the full picture."
