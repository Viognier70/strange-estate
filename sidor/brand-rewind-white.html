          Beskrivning

          Music Pairing:

          Product type: Spritzer (wine-based), in a can (250 ml)

          Sensory profile: Dry, lightly sparkling spritzer in an elegant, grape-authentic style with notes of yellow apples and white stone fruit. On the palate, gently effervescent with hints of lime and citrus, finishing elegantly dry with balanced freshness.

          Pairing suggestions: Suitable as an aperitif, pre-dinner drink, or pre-dinner cocktail. Ideal for outdoor and indoor serving, concerts, and festivals. Best served with fish and seafood dishes, such as seared scallops, as well as vegetarian and mildly spicy dishes.

          Curiosity / background: Made from 100% rescued Chardonnay wine from Northern Italy, upcycled in Stockholm. 100% handcrafted and blended, with natural flavoring from fresh lemon and lime juice, carbonated water, and syrup (natural aromas, sweetened with stevia).

          Origin: Stockholm (Sweden), Veneto (Italy)

          Sugar content: >0.05 g/L

          Alcohol content: 6.5% vol. alc.
